About Art From Earth


Art From Earth was founded with a mission to make an impact on the environment, but in a way that's creative.
We travel across Taiwan, picking up discarded trash and turning it into unique, meaningful art.
Through our exhibitions, we showcase how waste can be transformed into something beautiful in an artistic way.
We believe that art can inspire people to think differently about sustainability and contribute to positive change.
